Lost In Tropez

Lost in Tropez is an indie dance duo whose captivating sound seamlessly blends whimsical and gritty elements. Their journey began on the sun-soaked beaches of Southern California, where Glenn and Arsalan crossed paths during their school days. Drawing on their backgrounds in guitar and piano, they ventured into the realm of electronic production, honing their craft and forging a unique sonic identity together.

Lost in Tropez finds inspiration in a diverse range of musical influences, from the driving melodies of deep house to intense trap beats. This artistic juxtaposition highlights the power of collaboration, as these individual inspirations converge to create something entirely new. Their latest release, "Pieces," was born from the duo's exploration of San Diego's vibrant music scene, capturing the essence of freedom, solitude, and connection with their audience.

With their debut studio EP, "Ocean Views," Lost in Tropez demonstrated their ability to craft compelling melodies and lyrics. Many of the songs on this record were written and recorded using just an iPhone and a childhood guitar, showcasing their unwavering dedication to their art. "Pieces" represents a significant evolution in their sound, as they continue to push boundaries and pioneer a fresh approach to mainstream EDM.

The enchanting track "Pero te Dije" stands out as a guitar-driven gem on the EP, recounting Glenn's journey to reunite with Arsalan and reignite their creative spark. It was during this pivotal moment that the duo found themselves perched on the rooftop of Arsalan's apartment, armed with a laptop, keyboard, and guitar. The result was a sonic masterpiece and one of their proudest achievements to date.

"Pieces" the EP's lead single, draws inspiration from musical luminaries such as Polo and Pan, The Avalanches, and Romy. The track serves as a testament to Lost in Tropez's ability to blend cultures and sounds from North, Central, and South America, fostering unity through their music both in its creation and consumption.

While juggling full-time careers, music, and graduate school, Lost in Tropez remains committed to dedicating more time and energy to their craft. Their plans for the upcoming year include captivating live performances, organizing a tour, and returning to the studio to record their highly anticipated second full-length album. Stay tuned as Lost in Tropez continues to craft a sonic landscape that transcends boundaries and captivates listeners worldwide.

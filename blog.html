25:52



Junak is our newest and last single off our first full length album, 25:52. It brings together sounds from Latin America and French house to create a driving sound that leaves you wanting more.

Junak has been recognized and selected for several prominent playlists and blogs and we are excited to showcase our new direction and sound. Ocean Views joined together for the first time two independent artists, Ope and R.C. along with fusing their deep house and trap tendencies. Our first album sees the emergence of a new sound that brings together fun, carnival like sounds with smooth melodies and powerful vocals.

We have previously been showcased on several blogs and one of our singles, Pieces, was selected to be a part of the wonderful charity playlist Opportunities where all proceeds were sent to charity.
